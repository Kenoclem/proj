ABOUT US:

GCPI is a family corporation established in 2010 with the objective to publish quality textbooks and other learning materials. We have been privileged to be a provider of textbooks to institutions like the Manila Ecclesiastical Province School Systems Association (MaPSA), Don Bosco Technical Institute Makati, La Consolacion College Caloocan, La Consolacion College Deparo, St. James Academy Quezon City, the Batangas State University, The University of Cagayan Valley, and Assumption College Makati and other CEAP Schools in Luzon and Visayas.
